ABOUT KENNAN HODOVIC

Kennan Hodovic was born in May 2001 in Houston, Texas and spent most of his time growing up reading books. Known as the one kid in school who carried tomes in his bag, he loved reading epic fantasy series and was never daunted by the size of books or how many in a series there were. It wasn't until Freshman year of college that he learned he had a passion for writing and started creating stories of his own that he wrote on the side while studying aerospace engineering at Texas A&M University. The stories he imagined kept getting longer and longer until eventually he had the plans for his own epic fantasy series. The first book in that series, Sins of Ash, was written Senior year in 2023 and its sequel, Tears of Thunder, in 2024. Now he is working on publishing his debut novel and hopes to share these books with the world soon.
